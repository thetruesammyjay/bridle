SECURITY

Encrypted KeyVault

Keys never exist in plaintext at rest. Bridle ensures every agent's wallet is completely isolated and secure.

🛡️ AES-256-GCM encryption
🔑 PBKDF2 derived keys (100k iterations)
🗑️ Secure deletion & wiping from memory

GUARDS

Policy Guards

Even intelligent AI can make mistakes. Bridle wraps agents in strict boundaries to prevent catastrophic losses.

Limits & Caps

Per-agent spending limits and daily maximum transaction caps.

Token Whitelists

Agents can only trade approved tokens (SOL, USDC, BONK, etc).

Cooldown Periods

Enforced minimum time between trades to prevent frantic trading.

Immutable Audit

Append-only JSONL logs for every single decision, trade, and error.
